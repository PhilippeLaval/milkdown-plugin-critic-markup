## Comment with question

The fox ran at 45 mph.{>>Is this accurate? I thought foxes max out at 30 mph.<<}

## Comment with suggestion

The quick brown fox jumped.{>>Consider using more descriptive language here.<<}

## Multiple comments in one paragraph

The fox{>>Which fox are we talking about?<<} jumped over the fence{>>Add description of the fence.<<} and ran away.
