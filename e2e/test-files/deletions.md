# Deletion Tests

## Single word deletion

The {--quick--} fox jumped.

## Multi-word deletion

The fox {--jumped over the fence and--} ran away.

## Deletion at start of sentence

{--In the beginning, --}the fox appeared.

## Deletion at end of sentence

The fox ran away{-- into the darkness--}.

## Multiple deletions in one paragraph

The {--old--} fox {--slowly--} walked {--carefully--} through the forest.

## Deletion with punctuation

The fox said{--, "Goodbye world!"--} and stayed.

## Adjacent deletions

The{-- quick--}{-- brown--} fox jumped.

## Deletion of entire phrase

{--This entire sentence should be removed.--}

- {--Remove this item--}
