# Insertion Tests

## Single word insertion

The {++quick++} fox jumped.

## Multi-word insertion

The fox {++jumped over the fence and++} ran away.

## Insertion at start of sentence

{++Once upon a time, ++}the fox appeared.

## Insertion at end of sentence

The fox ran away{++ into the sunset++}.

## Multiple insertions in one paragraph

The {++brown++} fox {++quickly++} jumped {++high++} over the lazy dog.

## Insertion with punctuation

The fox said{++, "Hello world!"++} and left.

## Insertion with special characters

Add the {++@deprecated++} annotation and the {++#TODO++} comment.

## Adjacent insertions

The{++ quick++}{++ brown++} fox jumped.

## Insertion in a list

- First item
- {++New item added++}
- Last item

## Insertion in bold context

The **{++very important++}** fox jumped.
